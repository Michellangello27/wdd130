Urubamba River

Rafting Routes on the Urubamba River The Urubamba River meanders through the Sacred Valley of Peru and offers exciting whitewater rafting. It has two places for rafting, near the towns of Ollantaytambo and Huaran. These places are perfect for rafting lovers.

Ollantaytambo River

Ollantaytambo, in the heart of the Sacred Valley, is a privileged place to practice rafting on the Urubamba River. This area has a two-hour float with Class II and III sacred valley rafting rapids. You will see stunning views of Veronica Mountain and the Inca fortress of Ollantaytambo.
