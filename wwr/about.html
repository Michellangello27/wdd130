Nature and Adventure

If you have an adventurous spirit, enjoy exciting experiences and are someone in search of experiencing adrenaline to the surface, strong emotions, adrenaline and being in contact with nature and at the same time enjoying beautiful natural landscapes, you will love living and practicing rafting.

This sport consists of descending a river aboard a rowing boat, going through various tests, such as rocks that appear on the way, unevenness and turbulence produced by the current.

History

The unevenness of the rivers causes the boat to slide at high speed over the water, turning rafting into a somewhat risky activity. In fact, there is an international scale to measure the intensity of rapids, which ranges from I to VI, although tourist routes are usually class I to III and the following are considered for experts. Considering that we like this type of adventure, it is important that you use the safety equipment

provided to you at all times (helmet, vest, in some cases special clothing for cold water, such as a wetsuit). Above all, it is important that you take into account the advice they give you so that, working as a team, you have a great experience in beautiful natural settings. Start preparing your swimsuit, your sunscreen, your wet shoes, and hold on tight!
